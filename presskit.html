Rise of Piracy Press Kit

For Media Usage

Become the commander. Lead your fleet. Conquer new lands.

After 4 years in development from a solo/indie developer - Rise of Piracy brings you a fresh take on the Action/RTS genre, bridging the gap between classic titles such as Sid Myers Pirates! & Mount and Blade. Promising multiple campaigns filled with dynamic events and unique randomness, you can build your own story. Choose to trade, grow your fleet using conventional means, or turn to Piracy and take all you can.
