Rise of Piracy Press Kit

For Media Usage

Become the commander. Lead your fleet. Conquer new lands.

After 4 years in development from an indie solo developer - Rise of Piracy brings you a fresh take on the Action/RTS genre, bridging the gap between classic titles such as Sid Myers Pirates! & Mount and Blade. Promising multiple campaigns filled with dynamic events and unique randomness, you can build your own story. Choose to trade, grow your fleet using conventional means, or turn to Piracy and take all you can.

Gameplay

Rise of Piracy focuses heavily on progression and growth. In your campaign, you will first be given a small ship and a handful of coin. Complete missions and find treasure to build your economic strength and relations with the other factions. You can also choose to fight, boarding or sinking other vessels for loot. Build up your fleet of ships and their crews to take on the most challenging tasks such as capture islands of various strength and composition. Defeating large colonial fleets in grand scale naval warfare. Hunt pirates or long lost treasures. Uncovering mysterious lands filled with dangers as you go.

Campaigns

Hundreds of hand-crafted environments, no procedural generation. Everything in Rise of Piracy is carefully crafted to be a new experience and challenge. Fully destructible environments and buildings allow you to level an island from afar via bombardment with your fleet. Talk to mayors of settlements and captains of fleets to uncover hidden lands, or find them yourself through exploration and investigation.

A progression focused sandbox

Piracy is optional. Choose to trade with over 200 commodities that can be found scattered around the world. Ports with specialized trading routes and dedicated merchants to trade with. Add trading ships to your fleet to maximize your transportation potential. Build relations with other factions to increase your trading effectiveness. Engaging in war, hunting bounties, or seeking hidden treasures are also viable means of building your bank.

Alliances and Trade

A deep diplomacy system allows relationships to grow between all factions. Engage in diplomacy with every faction you meet. AI factions will go to war with one another and their assets will change hands. Changing the balance of powers can impact your play-style, increasing warship activity, and blocking trade routes.

Content

Fill your fleet with over 30 playable ships from 1600 - 1900, from the light and fast Carousel to the tremendous firepower but limited maneuverability that comes with the 120 Gun ship of the Line. Taverns provide a great place to hire crewmen for your fleet. Fill your ships with specialized crew. Use them to board hostile ships, or invading and capturing islands to secure passive income. Hostile actions will have consequences amongst the factions - fleets of pirate hunters are constantly seeking bounties. Campaign difficulty will grow dynamically and remain challenging no matter how strong players get.

Multiplayer

Take to one of the arenas to battle in team based or individual combat. Sink your enemies using a choice of ships with varying strengths and weaknesses.

About the developer

Starting as a hobby in 2017, Irish John Games picked up Unity3D for the first time after a career in software development. Soon the enjoyment of creation turned to passion and his ambition grew to one day releasing a game he had been dreaming about since his teenage years.

Inspired by classics such as Sid Meyers: Pirates, Cutthroats: Terror on the High Seas, and Mount and Blade - John aimed to merge the best pieces of these games. Rise of Piracy has a unusual mix of RTS and 3rd person controls to do battle, with the added RPG style mixed into the single player campaigns.

Shortly after watching many hours of Game Development streamers on twitch, John decided to start streaming the development. The added insights and assistance that the twitch game development community aided him in creating the first draft of what would later become Rise of Piracy. After restarting from scratch 3 times - Rise of Piracy was finally ready for the Steam Store.

Release

Rise of Piracy will enter Early Access in Q4 2022 exclusivly for Windows. This will allow development to continue in a community focused method, as it has been developed.

Initially, Rise of Piracy will release with a strong focus on Single Player campaigns and scenarios. A small Player vs Player multiplayer mode will also feature in the initial release.

The plan after early access is live, is to listen to the players and decide on features requested. Prioritising based off feedback.

The price has been set initially to $17.99* with a discount percentage on release.

Future releases will include more campaigns, historic missions and cooperative play. More language support. There may also be added platform support if demand is high enough.
